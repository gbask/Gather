HEY! Awesome to see you here. I’m Joanna, Creator of Gather, and this is my main squeeze, Graham.

We love being in love, and want to stay that way.

We know that it takes a village – having great friends and other awesome people in our lives helps keep our relationship strong.

But just because we’re in a relationship, shouldn’t mean that we won’t meet new awesome people unless we move, start new jobs, or get really awkward trying to strike up a conversation with a couple we come across out and about. Especially considering that life happens – friends get married, move on, move away, have kids. And we all know that walk of life is an important part of how well we can relate to other.

Life’s too short to go at it as a lone pair. Let’s go to-gather.
